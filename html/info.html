New Homes

What are we?

New Homes is a web platform for finding a best possible new home for your best friend - or to give one to someone! New Homes is user friendly and easy to use site, where by creating a profile you can connect with other dog lovers.

How to find a dog

If you are looking for to adopt a dog, first you need to create an account. Once you're in, you are able to search (with different criteria) a new dog-friend from our platform. If you find a pontential dog, send a message to the owner, and just wait.

In short for adopter

1. Register and create an account to yourself
2. Once your in, browse dogs
3. Found one? Contact the owner!
4. If it's a match, take care of all the paperwork with the owner. NewHomes is only a platform for uploading posts.
5. You have a new friend!



In short for owner

1. Register and create an account for yourself
2. Go to your profile, and create a new post of your dog. Once it's created, it will appear to the front page and can be found trough the search.
3. Wait for potential adopters
4. Once you have found one, take care of the paperwork with the adopter. NewHomes is only a platform for uploading posts.
5. It's done!
